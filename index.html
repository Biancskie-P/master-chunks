About Us

Master Chunks is a small business based in Quezon City, established in 2022, and passionate about creating delicious, freshly baked treats made with care and quality ingredients. Each product is crafted to bring comfort, joy, and sweet moments to everyone who enjoys them.

About the Owner

Kath Pacoma

She is the heart behind Master Chunks. The business was established during the pandemic in 2022, inspired by the idea that people were spending more time at home and relying heavily on food delivery services. With this in mind, Master Chunks aims to create delicious baked products that people can enjoy in the comfort of their homes or anywhere they choose.
